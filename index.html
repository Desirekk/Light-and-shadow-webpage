Light & Shadow (Working Title)

A mobile idle RPG in active development by Grindare Studio, built in Godot 4.5 with a Microsoft PlayFab backend. The project focuses on long-term progression, retention-driven systems, and competitive play.

Android & iOS Work in Progress / Pre-Alpha Casual / Idle RPG

Gameplay Video

This video demonstrates the current build’s core systems: UI flow, asynchronous PvE activities, combat resolution, equipment progression, and early competitive elements.

Key gameplay systems are visible within the first minute of the video.

  • Activity selection → combat → rewards → upgrades → repeat
  • Designed for short sessions with long-term progression
  • Backend-supported progression via PlayFab

Current Build

  • Playable mobile build with core gameplay loop implemented
  • Asynchronous PvE hunts and missions
  • Equipment progression and stat growth
  • Early PvP and ranking systems
  • Foundation prepared for live-ops iteration

Major Systems in Development

  • Guilds – social progression, upgrades, roles, economy
  • Guild Wars – large-scale PvP (member vs member)
  • Dungeons / lochs – repeatable multi-stage PvE content
  • Premium shop & monetisation – premium currency, cosmetics, subscriptions and seasonal battle pass, designed to be fair and non-pay-to-win
  • Balancing, QA hardening and structured external testing
  • Consistent visuals, animations and sound design

6-Month Funding Plan

  • Months 1–2: Stabilisation, bug fixing, UI/UX polish, visuals & sound integration
  • Months 3–4: Guild systems and progression, integration testing
  • Months 5–6: Dungeons, guild wars, premium shop foundations, balancing, external testing

By the end of the funding period, the project is expected to reach a closed beta or soft-launch ready state.

How the Funding Will Be Used

  • Full-time development to complete core systems
  • Visual, animation and audio polish
  • Testing, balancing and performance optimisation

Funding will directly enable completion of core multiplayer, progression and monetisation foundations required for beta readiness.
