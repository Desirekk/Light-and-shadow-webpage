About the Game

Grant-ready overview
Summary

Light & Shadow is a mobile idle RPG built for long-term progression and competitive rankings. Players choose a class (Warrior, Mage or Archer), complete asynchronous PvE hunts, and improve their character through equipment upgrades and materials. Competitive PvP arenas and global leaderboards provide ongoing goals, while social systems such as guilds extend retention through group progression.

The project is backend-driven (PlayFab) and designed around live systems such as daily resets, rotating content pools and scalable worlds/servers. The current build is functional and ready for beta/soft-launch testing, with the next development phase focused on content expansion, balancing, visual polish, onboarding improvements and release readiness.

Key Features
  • Asynchronous PvE hunts with stamina loop and rotating biomes
  • PvP arena with rating, matchmaking and global leaderboards
  • Deep equipment system (8 slots) with upgrades and materials
  • Daily quests, login streak rewards and repeatable progression
  • Guild system with roles, progression buildings and social features
  • Live-ops ready structure: resets, rotations and scalable worlds
  • Backend-driven persistence and validation (PlayFab + CloudScript)
